01 Overview

The GeeksforGeeks CU Chapter Website is a central platform created to represent the GFG student community at Chandigarh University. The website showcases the chapter's mission, upcoming events, team members, blogs, coding resources, and student achievements, all under one visually engaging and informative platform.

02 The Challenge

While student chapters play a major role in creating learning communities, most lack an official digital presence. Without a central place for announcements, event registration, or showcasing community work, outreach and participation often remain limited.

03 Our Solution

This project provides an elegant and informative website tailored for the GeeksforGeeks CU Chapter, aiming to:

  • Promote GFG CU events, workshops, and hackathons
  • Introduce the core team and club mentors
  • Share coding resources, blogs, and success stories
  • Allow students to connect, learn, and participate actively
  • Strengthen the visibility of the chapter within and outside the university

04 Technology Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Design: Figma / Canva (for visual planning)

Animations: CSS/JS effects (scroll reveal, hover cards, particle.js)

Deployment: GitHub Pages / Netlify
